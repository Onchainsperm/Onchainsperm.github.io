What is ONCHAIN SPERM?

The ONCHAIN SPERM project has two main components: the ONCHAIN SPERM NFT and the SPERMO TOKEN.

ONCHAIN SPERM NFT

Is a unique digital asset that is stored on the blockchain. It is designed to provide value to its holders by generating SPERMO TOKENs every day. These tokens are distributed as a reward to NFT holders, incentivizing them to hold onto their NFTs for a longer period of time.

SPERMO TOKEN

Is a cryptocurrency that was launched with a fair distribution model, meaning that no tokens were pre-mined before the launch. The total maximum supply of SPERMO TOKENs is 21,000, which means that the token is deflationary by design.

The SPERMO TOKEN can be used for a variety of purposes, including trading, staking, and providing liquidity to decentralized exchanges (DEXs).
